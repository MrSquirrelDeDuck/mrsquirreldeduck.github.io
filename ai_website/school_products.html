Frequently Asked Questions

How much does delivery cost?

Delivery is free for orders over $300.

For orders below that, there is a standard delivery fee of $10.

How quickly will my order be delivered?

We aim to process and dispatch your order within 24 hours.

You should then receive your order within 2-3 days.

What happens if I'm not home when my order arrives?

If you're not home when your order arrives, we'll leave it in a safe place.

If there is no safe place at the delivery address, the courier will leave a card and you'll have to contact them to arrange another delivery.

How can I pay for my order?

We accept payment by credit card and PayPal.

If you prefer you can pay via direct deposit or cheque.
